Why it exists

Story

AIppocampus began from a simple discomfort: a new thread can be technically brilliant and still lose the human continuity of the work.

Fresh context should be a baseline, not a fate.

The project is not trying to make a model pretend it has an inner autobiography. It gives the next agent a reliable way to find the source trail: old corrections, unfinished work, route context, style preferences, reading notes, and the thread of a relationship that should not vanish just because the chat window changed.

That is why the language stays careful. Source-backed continuity is not innate memory. It is the practice of reopening evidence so the present can continue honestly.

Where the deeper writing lives.

The website keeps the story short. The source essays and living threads remain in the repository and Discussions where their evidence, comments, and context are still visible.
